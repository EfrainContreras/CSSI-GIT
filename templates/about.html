About

Who is Meet2Eat

Meet2Eat was created by three Google CSSI students: Carolina, Efrain, and Vanessa. This idea came to us when we thought of some problems that people have everyday. The problem that we saw was that many people eat alone. Some reasons are because people are very shy and antisocial or simply because they couldn't find a group to go out to eat with. Our team created this app to allow people to meet others. Our objective is to get people to make more relationships and to end the fear of eating out alone.
