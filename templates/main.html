Who

Anyone can use Meet2Eat ... In fact, EVERYONE is encouraged to do so!

This app was made for anyone who ever wanted to share a meal, whether it be with friends or a stranger. Meet2Eat's design is intentionally versatile enough for use in a variety of scenarios. Use your imagination, and picture all your possibilities.

What

In order to give you the perfect social dining experience, Meet2Eat looks at the following categories:

  • Location
  • Date and Time
  • Food Preference
  • Number in Party
  • Additional Information (e.g. gender, hobbies, age etc.)

Our Vision

Here at Meet2Eat, we believe that meeting more people & forming new relationships make life truly unique. By using our app, you're entering a new network with other friends who also appreciate the importance and meaning of eating together. So, go ahead, share a meal, and live life to the fullest.
